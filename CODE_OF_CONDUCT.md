# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want this project to be a respectful, inclusive, and productive community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in discussion and review
- Focus on ideas, not individuals
- Provide constructive, actionable feedback
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- Personal attacks or intimidation
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this policy.

## Reporting

If you encounter unacceptable behavior, open a private security/community report as described in `SECURITY.md`.
